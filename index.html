Only For You ❤️

Malik Habib Sultan

Why do you think I would get bored of you?

You stay in my heart in a way words can’t explain…

Some feelings are so deep… I don’t know how to express them.

Sometimes I wish you could understand without me saying anything.

It’s okay… really.

You are a good person.

Some people are just special, and you will always remain one of them.

Your eyes… I don’t even know how to describe them properly.

They feel calm… unforgettable…


End ❤️
